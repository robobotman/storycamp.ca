About Story Camp

Story Camp is a creative summer camp based in Ontario, Canada. We help young creatives express their ideas through engaging storytelling workshops, whether they dream of writing books or making movies.

Our hands-on programs are designed to foster imagination, creativity, and collaboration, empowering campers to craft stories that will last a lifetime.

Our Programs

Book Creation

Our Book Creation stream helps young authors learn the process of writing, illustrating, and publishing their own stories. Campers will take home their very own published book at the end of the program.

Movie Making

In the Movie Making stream, campers learn how to script, direct, and film their own short movies. They'll develop skills in storytelling, cinematography, and teamwork to create their masterpiece.
