Let's Make A Storybook

At Story Camp, campers will have the opportunity to create a fully bounded story book in one week. The story-making process will be guided by a New York Times published children's book author Susan Yoon.


Book Making

We are a brand new camp. Our goal is to offer young capable hands the opportunity to learn by doing. The approach is to bring together real-world professional artists to show and teach your children the art of storytelling. We will start with book making.

This is not a DIY camp. This is a camp that will accellerate learning by allowing the campers to have direct insight from those currently in the publishing industry. We are professional writers, illustrators, and artists.

Each day, campers will learn about the different pillars of the storytelling process. Starting with ideation, to the binding of the book. It will be a 5 day, intensive process that will push the campers to create a book that they will be very proud of.

1. Ideation

Explore storytelling by building a strong framework.

2. Story Writing

Learn techniques to write engaging stories.

3. Storyboarding

Bring your story to life with illustrations.

4. Illustrate

Bring your story to life with illustrations.

5. Colour

Bring your story to life with illustrations.

6. Bind

Bring your story to life with illustrations.
