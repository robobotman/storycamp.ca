About Story Camp

Let’s make books! At Story Camp, the children will have an opportunity to ideate, draft, draw, and create a full bounded book in one week, directly learning from an up-and-coming author who has published two children’s books with Macmillan Publishing and Abrams Books (Waiting for Tomorrow, Gwendolyn and the Light).

Story Camp will offer a full day summer camp from 8:45 AM to 3:45 PM that will allow the campers to not only learn from real authors and illustrators but also from each other by sharing ideas and sometimes “stealing like an artist.”

The 5-day program will involve a simplified writing program from beginning to end that will provide the complete workflow to take an idea to a physical book. At the end of the camp week, each child will have the ability to share a fully bound book with their friends and family. Maybe even publish it directly through a children’s publishing program (in development with a Canadian publisher).

This is a one-of-a-kind camp by real Toronto artists and publication professionals to give your child the space to create.
